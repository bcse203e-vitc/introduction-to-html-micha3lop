The Alchemist is a novel by Paulo Coelho that has been translated into more than 80 languages and has sold more than 65 million copies worldwide. The story follows a young shepherd named Santiago. He has a recurring dream of finding treasure at the Pyramids in Egypt. He sets out on a journey from Spain to Egypt to fulfill his personal legend.

     Santiago meets various people on his journey who teach him valuable lessons about life, love, and spirituality. He also faces numerous challenges and setbacks, but he perseveres and learns to listen to his heart.
